## 2. Personal Data We Collect

- **Account data:** first name, last name, email, phone number, country and password (your password is stored irreversibly hashed, never in plain text).
- **Membership & usage data:** your plan/package, remaining credits, designs you generate, favorites and in-app activity records.

- **Technical data:** session token, device/browser information and error logs (for security and improvement).
- **Support messages:** messages and contact details you send us via Help/Support.

## 3. Purposes of Processing

- To create and manage your account and verify your identity (OTP/email).
- To generate AI-powered nail designs and provide the service.
- To handle subscription/payment operations.
- To ensure security, prevent fraud and improve the service.
- To comply with legal obligations and respond to your support requests.

## 4. Legal Bases

We process your data based on: performance of a contract (providing the service), your explicit consent (e.g. camera use), legitimate interest (security and improvement) and legal obligation.

## 5. Sharing With Third Parties

We use a limited number of service providers (sub-processors) to deliver the service:

- **AI image generation:** a text-based request is sent to an AI provider (e.g. Replicate / Flux). Your hand photo is not sent.
- **Payment:** payment provider (e.g. iyzico / Stripe / Merchant of Record).
- **SMS and email:** providers used for verification codes and notifications.

These providers only access the data necessary for their task and are bound by data-processing agreements. We never sell your data.

## 6. International Data Transfer

Some of our providers' servers may be located abroad (e.g. the USA). In such cases your data may be transferred internationally under appropriate safeguards (e.g. standard contractual clauses).

## 7. Retention

We keep your personal data while your account is active and for applicable legal retention periods. When you delete your account, your data is permanently deleted; a limited re-registration block (40 days) may apply to prevent abuse.

## 8. Your Rights

Under applicable law (KVKK / GDPR) you have the right to access, correct, delete, object to processing, data portability and to withdraw consent. You can exercise these rights via the **Delete Account** and **Help/Support** features in the App, or by writing to l.shalili@logper.com.

## 9. Data Security

We apply technical and organizational measures such as encryption (HTTPS), password hashing and access controls. No system is 100% secure, but we take reasonable care to protect your data.

## 10. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for anyone under 18. If we learn that a user under 18 has provided us data, we will delete it.
